## Work Culture & Management
### General
- `01 min` | 📄 | **[What I Wish Someone Had Told Me](https://blog.samaltman.com/what-i-wish-someone-had-told-me)** | *Sam Altman*
  Some notes about leading an organization.
- `03 min` | 📄 | **[It matters who does science](https://www.science.org/content/blog-post/it-matters-who-does-science)** | *Holden Torp*
  About seeing scientists as people and diversity in science, by the Editor-in-Chief of Science journals. 
- `05 min` | 🧵 | **[The Tao of Cheese](https://twitter-thread.com/t/1432654418437095431)** | *Iain Cheeseman*
  Words of wisdom for how to navigate science and your career.
- `05 min` | 📄 | **[San Francisco Declaration on Research Assessment (DORA)](https://sfdora.org/read/)** | *DORA initiative*
  Proposal for improving how the outputs of scholarly research and scientists are evaluated beyond journal-based metrics.
- `06 min` | 📄 | **[A person before a PhD: understanding and combatting an academic identity crisis](https://www.nature.com/articles/d41586-020-00306-y)** | *Robert Seaborne*
  How overcommitment to PhD work can lead to a loss of identity and three strategies to rediscover oneself.
- `09 min` | 📄 | **[Health tips for research groups](https://www.nature.com/articles/d41586-018-05146-5)** | *Norris et al.*
  Nature asked scientists to recommend one thing that institutional and laboratory leaders could do to make science more productive, rigorous and happy.
- `10 min` | 📄 | **[How lab heads can learn to lead](https://www.nature.com/articles/d41586-018-05156-3)** | *Roberta Kwok*
  Leadership experts translate business and coaching principles into advice for PIs on running healthier, more effective and supportive research groups.
- `11 min` | 📄 | **[The Thrill of Defeat](https://web.archive.org/web/20240914025143/https://nautil.us/the-thrill-of-defeat-235312/)** | *Bob Goldstein*
  What the stories of Francis Crick and Sydney Brenner can teach us about being "scooped" and open science.
- `16 min` | 📄 | **[Ten simple rules towards healthier research labs](https://journals.plos.org/ploscompbiol/article?id=10.1371/journal.pcbi.1006914)** | *Fernando Maestre*
  10 simple rules to make the working environment of research labs more nurturing, collaborative, and people-centered.
- `16 min` | 📄 | **[Ten simple rules to cultivate belonging in collaborative data science research teams](https://journals.plos.org/ploscompbiol/article?id=10.1371/journal.pcbi.1010567)** | *Gaynor et al.*
  Ideas to increase inclusiveness and belonging in teams (in data science, but applies more general).
- `20 min` | 📄 | **[Ten simple rules to improve academic work–life balance](https://journals.plos.org/ploscompbiol/article?id=10.1371/journal.pcbi.1009124)** | *Bartlett et al.*
  How to improve one’s own well-being, and how environments can promote well-being and so trainees can thrive and move forward in their careers.
- `20 min` | 📄 | **[A field guide to cultivating computational biology](https://journals.plos.org/plosbiology/article?id=10.1371/journal.pbio.3001419)** | *Way et al.*
  Biology in the digital era requires computation and collaboration. We propose a general solution: “deep integration” between biology and the computational sciences.
- `22 min` | 📄 | **[Science, narcissism and the quest for visibility](https://febs.onlinelibrary.wiley.com/doi/full/10.1111/febs.14032)** | *Bruno Lemaitre*
  About narcissistic personalities in science.
- `22 min` | 📑 | **[One hundred research rules of the game](https://www.emerald.com/aaaj/article-pdf/32/2/691/11751/aaaj-02-2019-032.pdf)** | *Niamh Brennan*
  100 "rules" for making your research "world class".
- `26 min` | 📚 | **[Advice for Graduate Students: The Stearns-Huey-Witz Series](https://stearnslab.yale.edu/modest-advice)** | *Stearns, Huey, Witz*
  Three connected essays, with some truths and insights into thinking of older generations: Stearns' original **[Modest Advice ('87)](https://stearnslab.yale.edu/modest-advice)**, Huey's **[Acynical Reply ('87)](https://esajournals.onlinelibrary.wiley.com/doi/pdf/10.2307/20166581)**, and Witz's **[Pragmatic Rebuttal ('94)](https://www.kevintshoemaker.net/pdf/Witz_grad_advice.pdf)**.
- `>60 min` | 📚 | **[How to grow a healthy lab](https://www.nature.com/collections/pmlcrkkyyq)** | *Nature*
  Collection of articles for a healthy research environment.

- `19 min` | 📄 | **[The Making of a Scientist](https://doi.org/10.1038/2151441a0)** | *Hans Krebs, 1967*

- `20 min` | 🎥 | **[Precision & Plasticity in Mentoring](https://youtu.be/rTYjbv9zmB0)** | *Angela DePace*
  Normalizing failure, leading by example, using conflict-management tools, and yearly development meetings. So trainees can thrive as whole people and stay in science.
