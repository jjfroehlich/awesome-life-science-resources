## Career Development

Resources for crafting applications, navigating the job market, and planning your career path both inside and outside of academia.

### CV & Resume Building
- [CV/Resume : Broad Institute of MIT and Harvard](http://mitcommlab.mit.edu/broad/commkit/cvresume/) - Guidance on building a CV or resume from the Broad Institute.
- [CVs | MIT Global Education & Career Development](https://gecd.mit.edu/jobs-and-internships/resumes-cvs-cover-letters-and-linkedin/cvs) - Resources and tips for creating CVs from MIT.
- [Academic Job Search - CV Guide (Part 1)](http://career.berkeley.edu/PhDs/PhDCV) - Berkeley's guide to starting an academic CV.
- [Academic Job Search - CV Elements (Part 2)](http://career.berkeley.edu/PhDs/PhDCVelements) - Berkeley's guide on the essential elements of an academic CV.
- [Resume Action Verbs](https://gecd.mit.edu/sites/default/files/jobs/files/resume-action-verbs.pdf) - A PDF list of powerful action verbs to make your resume stand out.
- [Europass](https://europass.cedefop.europa.eu/) - The official Europass website for creating a CV that is understood across Europe.
- [vitae: Dynamic CVs with R Markdown](https://ropensci.org/blog/2019/01/10/vitae/) - An R package to create and maintain CVs using R Markdown.
- [OpenResume](https://www.open-resume.com/) - A free, open-source resume builder and parser to help you create and tailor your resume.
- [Narrative CVs | Institute for Academic Development](https://institute-academic-development.ed.ac.uk/research-roles/research-only-staff/career-management/narrative-cvs) - An explanation and guide to creating narrative-style CVs.
- [We reviewed 10 of our subscribers' CVs!](https://www.youtube.com/watch?app=desktop&v=qXPJpnrfX_4) - A practical video review of real-life CVs with constructive feedback.

### Finding a Postdoc & Academic Positions
- [Tips for Getting a Faculty Position](http://blog.addgene.org/tips-for-getting-a-faculty-position) - Addgene blog post with advice for securing a faculty role.
- [Making the Right Moves | HHMI.org](http://www.hhmi.org/developing-scientists/making-right-moves) - A comprehensive guide for scientists on career development and lab management.
- [Tips for Young Scientists on the Junior Faculty Job Search](http://www.cell.com/neuron/fulltext/S0896-6273(17)30096-X) - An article in Neuron offering practical tips for the faculty job search.
- [Finding a lab to do your postdoc](https://docs.google.com/document/d/1-eXl9t95pzH0kZThYVSqxCOAqLkQSxgbZuSfeJmM2kc/edit) - A collaborative document with crowdsourced advice on finding a postdoc lab.
- [How to Pick a Graduate Advisor](https://www.cell.com/neuron/fulltext/S0896-6273(13)00907-0) - An article in Neuron on the crucial decision of choosing a graduate advisor.
- [Guide to a Postdoc](https://ubadahsabbagh.com/guide-to-postdoc.php) - A comprehensive personal guide on navigating the postdoc experience.
- [Academic job hunting: resources and advice](https://dimvladkost.github.io/resource/academic_jobs/) - A collection of resources and personal advice for academic job seekers.

### Interview & Application Advice
- [The Commandments of Cover Letter Creation](https://www.sciencemag.org/careers/1996/12/commandments-cover-letter-creation) - A classic article from Science on the dos and don'ts of cover letters.
- [50 COMMON INTERVIEW Q&A](https://bhuvans.wordpress.com/2006/08/19/50-common-interview-qa/) - A list of common interview questions and advice on how to answer them.
- [10 Most Asked Interview Questions: How to Tackle Them](https://www.hongkiat.com/blog/top-10-interview-questions/) - A guide to handling the most frequent interview questions.

## Funding & Grants

A collection of fellowship databases, specific grants, and resources for proposal writing.

### Grant Writing & Resources
- [Where to Search for Funding | Science | AAAS](http://www.sciencemag.org/careers/where-search-funding) - A guide to finding funding opportunities for your research.
- [Funding for independent postdoc fellowship in biology](https://docs.google.com/spreadsheets/d/1J2M_tQgoqV4ivpfEClh9qs9f2KQN2O1G3Jpda2aoHfQ/htmlview) - A community-curated spreadsheet of postdoc funding opportunities.
- [A list of publicly available grant proposals in the biological sciences](https://jabberwocky.weecology.org/2012/08/10/a-list-of-publicly-available-grant-proposals-in-the-biological-sciences/) - A collection of successful grant proposals shared publicly.
- [Open Grants](https://www.ogrants.org/) - A platform for sharing and viewing successful grant proposals.

### European Fellowships
- [EMBO Postdoctoral Fellowships](https://www.embo.org/funding/fellowships-grants-and-career-support/postdoctoral-fellowships/) - Prestigious postdoctoral fellowships offered by EMBO.
- [HFSP Postdoctoral Fellowships](https://www.hfsp.org/funding/hfsp-funding/postdoctoral-fellowships) - Fellowships for international research collaboration.
- [Marie Skłodowska-Curie Actions Postdoctoral Fellowships](https://ec.europa.eu/research/mariecurieactions/actions/postdoctoral-fellowships) - The EU's flagship fellowship program for researchers.
- [Leopoldina Postdoc Scholarship](https://www.leopoldina.org/foerderung/leopoldina-foerderprogramm/leopoldina-postdoc-stipendium/) - A scholarship for postdocs to conduct research at a German research institution (in German).

### German Fellowships
- [DFG Individual Research Grants](https://www.dfg.de/en/research_funding/programmes/individual/research_grants/index.html) - The primary funding instrument of the German Research Foundation.
- [Fritz Thyssen Stiftung Postdoc Stipends](https://www.fritz-thyssen-stiftung.de/foerderung/foerderarten/postdoc-stipendien/) - One-year postdoctoral scholarships (in German).
- [Daimler und Benz Stiftung - Postdoctoral program](https://www.daimler-benz-stiftung.de/cms/en/promote/scholarship-program/postdoctoral-program.html) - A scholarship program for postdocs in Germany.
- [Klaus Tschira Boost Fund](https://gsonet.org/funding-programs/klaus-tschira-boost-fund/?lang=en) - Funding to support early-career researchers in Germany.

## Research Guidance

Advice and frameworks for generating ideas, writing, and presenting scientific work.

### Finding Good Projects
- [The importance of stupidity in scientific research](https://jcs.biologists.org/content/121/11/1771) - An essay on embracing the feeling of being "stupid" as a natural part of research.
- [How to choose a good scientific problem](https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/pubmed/19782018) - A paper by Uri Alon on selecting impactful research questions.
- [A hypothesis is a liability | Genome Biology](https://genomebiology.biomedcentral.com/articles/10.1186/s013059-020-02133-w) - An argument for focusing on questions rather than being wedded to a single hypothesis.
- [Planning Your Scientific Journey • iBiology](https://www.ibiology.org/educators-resources/online-course/planning-your-scientific-journey/#) - An online course on how to plan and navigate a scientific career.
- [Make science disruptive again](https://www.nature.com/articles/s41587-023-01736-5) - An article advocating for more ambitious and disruptive scientific projects.

### Scientific Writing
- [Ten simple rules for structuring papers](http://journals.plos.org/ploscompbiol/article?id=10.1371/journal.pcbi.1005619) - A widely cited guide on how to structure a scientific paper.
- [The Science of Scientific Writing](https://www.americanscientist.org/blog/the-long-view/the-science-of-scientific-writing) - An article on how to write more clearly by understanding reader expectations.
- [CRediT - Contributor Roles Taxonomy](https://casrai.org/credit/) - A standard taxonomy for acknowledging author contributions.
- [A Guide to Responding to Scientific Peer Review](https://jef.works/blog/2020/06/17/responding-to-scientific-peer-review/) - Practical advice for crafting effective responses to reviewer comments.
- [How to write an abstract](https://jef.works/blog/2019/09/24/how-to-write-an-abstract/) - A concise guide on writing an effective abstract.

### Presentations & Communication
- [How to Give a Good Talk - ScienceDirect](https://www.sciencedirect.com/science/article/pii/S1097276509007424) - A guide from Cell Press on delivering effective scientific talks.
- [Ira Glass on Storytelling](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=5pFI9UuC_fc&feature=youtu.be) - A classic series of short videos on the art of storytelling.
- [How to design an award-winning conference poster](http://blogs.lse.ac.uk/impactofsocialsciences/2018/05/11/how-to-design-an-award-winning-conference-poster/) - Tips and tricks for creating an effective poster.
- [Chalk talks: how to avoid 10 pitfalls](https://sociobiology.wordpress.com/2016/10/11/chalk-talks-how-to-avoid-10-pitfalls/) - Advice for preparing and delivering a chalk talk for an academic interview.
- [Elevator Pitch : Broad Institute of MIT and Harvard](https://mitcommlab.mit.edu/broad/commkit/elevator-pitch/) - A guide to crafting a concise and compelling elevator pitch.

## Computational & Data Skills

Resources for learning programming, statistics, and data visualization for biological research.

### Learning Platforms & Guides
- [R for Data Science](https://r4ds.had.co.nz/) - A foundational book for learning data science with R and the Tidyverse.
- [Computational Genomics with R](http://compgenomr.github.io/book/) - A practical guide to computational genomics using R.
- [The Biologist’s Guide to Computing](http://book.biologistsguide2computing.com/en/stable/) - A book aimed at biologists who need to learn computational skills.
- [Modern Statistics for Modern Biology](https://www.huber.embl.de/msmb/) - A course and book on modern statistical methods for biological data.
- [A Quick Guide to Organizing Computational Biology Projects](https://journals.plos.org/ploscompbiol/article?id=10.1371/journal.pcbi.1000424) - A paper on best practices for organizing computational projects.

### Data Visualization
- [Fundamentals of Data Visualization](https://clauswilke.com/dataviz/index.html) - A comprehensive guide to the principles of data visualization.
- [From data to Viz](https://www.data-to-viz.com/) - A decision tree that helps you choose the right chart for your data.
- [Ten Simple Rules for Better Figures](https://journals.plos.org/ploscompbiol/article?id=10.1371/journal.pcbi.1003833) - A classic guide on creating effective scientific figures.
- [SuperPlots: Communicating reproducibility and variability](https://rupress.org/jcb/article/219/6/e202001064/151717/SuperPlots-Communicating-reproducibility-and) - A paper introducing "SuperPlots" to show data variability transparently.
- [Bioicons](https://bioicons.com/) - A library of free, high-quality science illustrations.
- [SciDraw](https://scidraw.io/) - A collection of templates and tools for creating scientific figures.

### Single-Cell & RNA-seq Analysis
- [scRNA-tools](https://www.scrna-tools.org/) - A database of software tools for single-cell RNA-seq analysis.
- [kallisto & bustools](https://www.kallistobus.tools/) - A fast and efficient pipeline for pre-processing single-cell RNA-seq data.
- [Seurat](http://satijalab.org/seurat/) - A popular R package for single-cell genomics analysis.
- [scVelo](https://scvelo.readthedocs.io/) - A Python package for inferring RNA velocity to study cellular dynamics.
- [Orchestrating Single-Cell Analysis with Bioconductor](https://osca.bioconductor.org/) - A comprehensive book on single-cell analysis using Bioconductor packages.

## Discipline-Specific Resources

### *C. elegans* Research
- [WormBook](http://www.wormbook.org/) - A comprehensive, open-access collection of reviews on *C. elegans* biology.
- [Caenorhabditis Genetics Center (CGC)](https://cgc.umn.edu/) - The central resource for *C. elegans* strains and genetic information.
- [WormBase](https://wormbase.org/) - The primary database for the genetics and genomics of *C. elegans*.
- [C. elegans Codon Adapter](http://worm.mpi-cbg.de/codons/cgi-bin/optimize.py) - A tool for optimizing gene sequences for expression in *C. elegans*.
- [VisCello: C. elegans Embryogenesis Visualizer](https://cello.shinyapps.io/celegans_explorer/) - An interactive browser for single-cell expression data in the *C. elegans* embryo.

## Lab Culture & Mentoring

Advice on fostering a healthy lab environment, mentoring, and maintaining well-being.

- [The key to a happy lab life is in the manual](https://www.nature.com/articles/d41586-018-06167-w) - Nature article advocating for the creation of a lab manual to set clear expectations.
- [Ten simple rules towards healthier research labs](https://journals.plos.org/ploscompbiol/article?id=10.1371/journal.pcbi.1006914) - A guide to fostering a positive and productive lab environment.
- [DORA – San Francisco Declaration on Research Assessment](https://sfdora.org/) - An initiative to improve how scholarly research outputs are evaluated.
- [How Life Sciences Actually Work](https://guzey.com/how-life-sciences-actually-work/) - An in-depth analysis of the realities of life science research.
- [A person before a PhD: understanding and combatting an academic identity crisis](https://www.nature.com/articles/d41586-020-00306-y) - An article on managing the personal challenges of a PhD.
